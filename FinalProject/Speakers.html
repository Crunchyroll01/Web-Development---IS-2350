Meet Our Speakers

Discover the visionaries shaping the future of urban design and sustainability.

Valeria Cifuentes

Valeria is a renowned expert in the field of sustainable architecture, with over 20 years of experience designing eco-friendly buildings around the world. Her innovative approach to integrating nature into urban spaces has earned her numerous awards and recognition in the industry.

Regina Tenedor

Regina is a leading voice in the field of urban planning, with a focus on creating inclusive and equitable cities. Her work has been instrumental in shaping policies that promote affordable housing, public transportation, and community engagement in urban development.

Jared Thompson

Jared is a passionate advocate for digital innovation and its role in shaping the future of cities. His research focuses on the intersection of technology and urban design, exploring how smart solutions can enhance the quality of life in metropolitan areas.
